New categories for Jackets and Sweaters üçÅ  

Just in time for fall! You can now tag your items as sweaters or jackets. 

No more 72-hour waiting period on offers

Buyers can make offers on your items as soon as they're posted. This has already led to more sales! 
 

New shipping prices

When you post, you'll now be asked to select the item weight and shipping cost. The new $4.25 option is a great way to increase sales of lighter items.
NOTE: The new low-price shipping option ($4.25) doesn't work with the free Priority Mail supplies at the post office. You can use any box or envelope you have, or our Shipping Kit.

Shipping is now added at checkout for buyers

Shipping used to be included in the price, which confused sellers since the item would be a higher price than they set. It also made offers confusing. Now the price you list is the price buyers see.
