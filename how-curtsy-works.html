Curtsy 101

Shop. Slay. Sell. Repeat.

Curtsy is an app that lets you buy and sell cute clothes. It’s a fun way to always have something new to wear. Sell your stuff, fund a new outfit, wear it then repeat.

We’re big on the resale -- when you buy items on Curtsy, they go into your closet so you can easily resell them in the future with one tap.

It’s 2018, honey. There’s no reason to hold onto clothes. You know you’re only going to wear it once anyways :)

How selling works

Curtsy is the easiest way to sell your cute clothes. We recommend selling the stuff that you’d still want to buy. Items that are on trend and in-season sell best.

View Seller FAQ
POST IN UNDER A MINUTE

Posting is quick and easy: we’ll ask you to upload photos, describe your item, and set your price. Not sure about price? Don’t worry! We’ll suggest a price based on the recently sold items with the same brand and condition.

Learn about posting

To ensure a good shopping experience for buyers on Curtsy, we require all listings have at least one photo of the item on a person.

SIMPLE SHIPPING

On Curtsy, buyers pay for shipping. The price of shipping is either $4.25 (if your item is under 1 lb), or $7.50 (more than 1 lb). You'll select the correct price when you post your item.

When you make a sale, we’ll email you a pre-paid shipping label. It will work with any box or envelope. If your label is for Priority Mail (over 1 lb), you can use free supplies at USPS. We also offer our Shipping Kit for $3.84.

You can give the package to your mailwoman, drop it any blue collection box, schedule a free package pickup, or take it to the post office.

Learn about shipping for sellers
INSTANT PAYOUTS

You’ll be paid when the tracking shows the item is shipped. The money will go into your Curtsy Wallet, and from there you can transfer instantly to your debit card or bank (takes less than 5 minutes) OR spend the money in the app :)

Learn about getting paid
OUR FEE

We keep a small portion of every sale that we use to run our platform. You’ll be able to see the exact amount we take when you post so there are no surprises.

How Buying Works

Curtsy is the #1 place to save on cute clothes. It's like Nordstrom or Revolve, but way cheaper. We recommend checking the app daily for new arrivals and “loving” items you like-- the good ones won’t be around for long!

View Buyer FAQ
FAST SHIPPING

We ask sellers to ship your item within 1-2 days of your order. Most items take 2 days in transit after they're shipped by the seller.

Need it soon? Feel free to message the seller before you buy and ask when they could ship it.

After your item is shipped you’ll get a text with a link to track it.

APPLE PAY & MORE

When you’re ready to buy, easily checkout using Apple Pay or any major credit or debit card. Payment is securely handled by Curtsy, and we hold the money until the seller ships your item.

Learn about checkout and payment
ASK QUESTIONS

There's a button to "Message seller" on every item. Most sellers reply in under an hour. Need help from Curtsy? We’ll message you when you sign up -- just reply to that message if you ever need help.

WORRIED ABOUT FIT?

While we don’t offer returns, we do move the item into your Curtsy closet after you buy so you can easily resell it with one tap.

Chances are if you bought something, someone else wants it to-- most items that are re-listed sell in under 48 hours.

BUYER GUARANTEE

We guarantee a full refund if there are any issues with the item that weren’t described in the listing. Our support team is available via chat and phone throughout your whole buying experience in case you need help.
