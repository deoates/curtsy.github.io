Ship your order in 3 steps:

  1. Print out the shipping label we email you.
  2. Pack your item in any box, or free supplies from USPS.
  3. Give your package to USPS.

There are 3 ways to give your package to USPS:

  1. Schedule a free pickup at usps.gov
  2. Drop it in any USPS collection box
  3. Take it to your nearest post office to USPS.

Tips on becoming a shipping goddess

Ship the order promptly

Buyers are happiest when their order ships out within two days. If you need more time, be sure to let the buyer know. Good communication and fast shipping will help you maintain your high seller rating.

USPS, not UPS

Our labels only work with the U.S. Postal Service — not UPS or Fedex.

Save your Amazon boxes

You can use any box you have on hand as long as your item is under 5 lbs.

Get free boxes

Did you know the USPS will mail free shipping supplies to your house? We recommend the padded envelope and Medium boxes.

Order free supplies

Don’t want to wait? You can find the same free supplies at your nearest post office — next time you’re there, grab a few to keep on hand.

Make sure to get the ones that work with Priority Mail. The "express" blue envelopes don't work.

Schedule a free pickup

Free package pickups from the USPS are by far the easiest way to ship your items. You can specify where you want the item picked up, and your mailman will take the item during your regular mail delivery.

Schedule a pickup

Use a blue collection box

We know you’ve seen them! The USPS has an incredible network of those blue collection boxes — if your item will fit, feel free to drop it in there.

Find a collection box

Going to the post office? Skip the line

With our label, there’s no need to wait in line. Just drop your package at the front desk. And on your way out, be sure to grab some free supplies for next time.

Shipping FAQs

Can I use my own box?

Yeah! As long as your item is under 5lbs, you can use any box. If your item is more than 5lbs, let us know.

When do I get paid for my order?

You’ll be paid when your item is delivered to the buyer. After that, you can transfer instantly to your debit card or bank account. Our Instant Transfers typically go through in under 5 minutes.

How long do I have to ship?

We ask that all sellers ship orders within 2 days.

I need more time to ship. What should I do?

We get it -- life can get busy! Make sure to let the buyer know when you plan on shipping it, and give her the option to cancel if the new ship date doesn’t work for them.

After I ship, how long does it take to get to the buyer?

We use the Priority Mail service, which takes between 1-3 days depending on location. The average transit time is 2 days, so an item shipped on Monday would be delivered on Wednesday, for example.

Can I ship it from UPS or Fedex?

Our labels only work with USPS for now. We may add additional carriers in the future.
