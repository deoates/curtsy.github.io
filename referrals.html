Can I earn credit from referring my friends?

Yeah! With Curtsy's new referral program you'll earn $5 for every friend that shops using your promo code. They'll also get $5 off their order.

How can I share my promo code?

There are two ways to share your code:

  1. After you post an item, you'll be prompted to share that item to your Insta story. We'll include your promo code at the bottom of that story.
  2. From your profile (the girl icon in the lower right), you'll see an option for "Give $5, Get $5," tap that to share your promo code with your friends.

How will I know if my friend uses my promo code to shop?

You'll get a notification when your friend used your code and the $5 credit was added to your Curtsy Wallet.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

Not at all-- share your code and your love for Curtsy with as many people as you like :)
